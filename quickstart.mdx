## Register and Log In

VEC Fleet provides a simple onboarding process. Follow these steps to create your account and access the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://app.vecfleet.com/register` and enter your company details.

    - Email: yourfleet@company.com
    - Company name: Your Fleet Management Inc.
    - Password: Choose a strong password

    Agree to terms and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from VEC Fleet. Click the link to confirm.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to `https://app.vecfleet.com/login`. Enter your credentials.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable two-factor authentication during first login for added security.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Navigation

Once logged in, explore the main dashboard. It displays real-time metrics for your fleet, including vehicle status, fuel efficiency, and upcoming maintenance.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Dashboard" icon="globe">

    | Section          | Purpose                          |
    |------------------|----------------------------------|
    | Vehicles         | View and manage your fleet       |
    | Reports          | Generate custom analytics        |
    | Maintenance      | Schedule and track services      |
    | Expenses         | Monitor fuel and repair costs    |
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Add Vehicles

Add your vehicles manually, via CSV upload, or API to start monitoring.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Vehicles" icon="truck">
    Click **Vehicles** in the sidebar, then **Add Vehicle**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="edit-3">
    Fill in:

    <ParamField path="licensePlate" param-type="string" required="true">
      License plate number (e.g., ABC-1234).
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="vin" param-type="string" required="true">
      Vehicle Identification Number.
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="make" param-type="string">
      Manufacturer (e.g., Volvo, Scania).
    </ParamField>

    <ParamField path="model" param-type="string">
      Model year and type.
    </ParamField>

    Click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install Tracker" icon="map-pin">
    Order GPS trackers from VEC Fleet or pair existing devices.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Bulk Import Options

For larger fleets, use these methods:

<CodeGroup tabs="CSV Upload,API">
  ```csv
  licensePlate,vin,make,model,odometer
  TRK-001,1HGCM82633A004352,Volvo,FH16,150000
  TRK-002,3VWFE21C77M036367,Scania,R450,98000
  ```
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.vecfleet.com/v1/vehicles', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': `Bearer YOUR_API_KEY`,
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      vehicles: [
        {
          licensePlate: 'TRK-001',
          vin: '1HGCM82633A004352',
          make: 'Volvo',
          model: 'FH16',
          odometer: 150000
        }
      ]
    })
  });
  ```
</CodeGroup>

<Callout kind="success">
  Your first vehicle appears on the dashboard within minutes. Real-time tracking begins after GPS installation.
</Callout>
