## Overview

VEC Fleet empowers fleet operators across Latin America to manage thousands of vehicles efficiently. You centralize maintenance scheduling, track fuel consumption, and monitor expenses in real-time through our intuitive SaaS platform. With oversight of over `70,000` vehicles regionally, VEC Fleet delivers actionable insights to boost productivity and slash operational costs.

## Get Started Quickly

Follow these steps to launch VEC Fleet for your operations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Create your account at `https://app.vecfleet.com/register` and verify your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Vehicles" icon="plus">
    Upload your fleet CSV or integrate via API.

    <CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,Python">
      ```javascript
      const response = await fetch('https://api.vecfleet.com/fleets', {
        method: 'POST',
        headers: { 'Authorization': `Bearer ${YOUR_API_KEY}` },
        body: JSON.stringify({ vehicles: [{ id: 'veh-123', model: 'Toyota Hilux' }] })
      });
      ```
      ```python
      import requests
      response = requests.post(
          'https://api.vecfleet.com/fleets',
          headers={'Authorization': f'Bearer {YOUR_API_KEY}'},
          json={'vehicles': [{'id': 'veh-123', 'model': 'Toyota Hilux'}]}
      )
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Alerts" icon="bell">
    Set thresholds for fuel efficiency and maintenance intervals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Dashboard" icon="activity">
    Access insights and start optimizing your fleet.
  </Step>
</Steps>
